# Welcome to Steel Hawks Mechanical

Welcome to the mechanical documentation for **FRC Team 2601 - Steel Hawks**! This section covers everything you need to know about designing, fabricating, and assembling the physical robot.

## Getting Started

No prior experience is required to join — but you will need patience and attention to detail. Mechanical is where the robot physically takes shape: the frame, the drivetrain, and every mechanism that scores points.

## Mechanical is Hands-On

This is the subteam where ideas become metal. You'll work with hand tools, power tools, and shop machines, and you'll learn how a part goes from a CAD model to a finished, mounted component.

That means:

- Measuring twice and cutting once
- Building to the dimensions in the CAD — not "close enough"
- Working safely around tools and machines at all times
- Assembling mechanisms that hold up to a full competition

## What You'll Learn

This curriculum will grow over time. Planned topics include:

- Shop safety and tool basics
- Reading a CAD model and a drawing
- Fasteners, bearings, and gears
- Drivetrain fundamentals
- Fabrication: cutting, drilling, tapping
- Assembly and tolerancing

## Ready to Start?

Work through the topics in the sidebar in order. When you finish a topic, talk to a lead on the mechanical subteam — they'll pair you with a build to practice on.
